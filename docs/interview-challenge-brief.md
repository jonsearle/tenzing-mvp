# AI-Powered Account Prioritisation Tool

## Technical Challenge

---

## Context

A Tenzing portfolio company generates large volumes of operational data across leads, accounts, support activity, revenue signals, and customer notes.

Leadership struggles to consistently answer:
- Which accounts need attention
- Where revenue may be at risk
- Where growth opportunities are emerging
- What action should be taken

---

## Your Task

Build a prototype internal tool that produces a clear, defensible, and actionable prioritisation of accounts for leadership to focus on.

### Requirements

- The output must go beyond dashboards or summaries
- The product should help leadership:
  - Decide who to focus on
  - Understand why
  - Know what should happen next

---

## Source Data

The dataset is provided as a single CSV file.

- Contains account-level records combining:
  - Structured operational signals
  - Recent text notes
- Includes:
  - Accounts
  - Leads
  - Support activity
  - Revenue metrics
  - Customer notes

### Notes

- You may:
  - Derive features
  - Create views
  - Build supporting tables
- The CSV must remain the core data source

---

## Minimum Requirements

- Include authentication
- Use the provided dataset
- Integrate an AI API (Application Programming Interface)
- Tackle account prioritisation directly

---

## Example User Flow

1. Sign in
2. View a portfolio overview
3. See prioritised accounts
4. Drill into an account
5. View reasoning and supporting evidence
6. See recommended next actions
7. Save or record decisions

---

## Evaluation Criteria

- Clarity and defensibility of prioritisation logic
- Quality of AI integration and whether it improves decisions
- Architecture decisions
- Handling of imperfect operational data
- Quality of reasoning explanations
- UX (User Experience) clarity for leadership use
- Trade-offs and overall system thinking

---

## Not Being Evaluated

- Pixel-perfect design
- Extensive data cleaning
- Production-level robustness beyond reasonable scope

---

  - Architecture decisions
  - How prioritisation works
  - How AI improves the workflow
  - Trade-offs
  - What you would build next
